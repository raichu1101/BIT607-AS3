About Us


Located in Kerikeri, the Bay of Islands. Haukai Restaurant is a newly opened restaurant driven by a passion for Modern Māori and Pākeha kai – the Māori word for food. Our chef curates an innovative menu which blends the culinary traditions of both Māori and Pākeha with local Northland ingredients, and we aim to provide a unique and wonderful dining experience to you.
